## About this Add-on

This extension is intended for better readability of German webpages which make overextended use of the so called "Binnen-I". Binnen-Is are a phenomenon in German language, intended to show gender-parity in written texts - however in the eyes of many ***it is cluttering [written language](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Written_language "Written language") in an unnecessary way & it's not part of common [spelling rules](http://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Spelling_rule "Spelling rule")***.
